Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 Announces Collab With Popular Horror Movie

By Mahjbeen — Published 1 hour ago

Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 has confirmed an upcoming collaboration with the recently released horror film, I Know What You Did Last Summer. The latest Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 collaboration offers a more grounded crossover than some found recently in the game.

In recent years, there have been tons of unique Call of Duty crossovers with other franchises, usually involving the purchase of microtransactions to get access to skins. This hasn’t always been popular with the Call of Duty community, with some feeling that these types of collaborations can ruin the game’s immersion. The recent Black Ops 6 American Dad crossover is a pretty extreme example of this. Others like the ability to play as their favorite characters from other franchises, but there’s certainly no agreed community consensus on the matter.

In a trailer posted online, Treyarch revealed that I Know What You Did Last Summer is coming to Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 in the future

“We know this isn’t what you expected — but it’s something we truly believe in.”

COD : Black Oops 6 is expected to launch in early 2025 for PC and next-gen consoles. More gameplay footage is anticipated during the upcoming Game Awards.
